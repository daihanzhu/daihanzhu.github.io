Strengthening the Community

The Arts Student Union, or ASU for short, is the official representative for all Arts Undergraduate Students at the University of Waterloo. We govern 27 Student Societies as well as empowering students in their educational, social, and community endeavours.

I proudly served as First Year Rep from 2015-2016 and VP Communications from 2016-2017.

Designs

Throughout the two years, I've designed countless event posters, logos, infographics, stickers, and even Snapchat filters.

From not knowing how to use illustrator to designing each poster in under an hour, I love having the freedom to experiment and create the look and feel of our branding going forward.

Engagement

I'm driven by metrics and I'm continuingly looking for ways data can be a compelling way to tell stories. I experimented with ways to attain feedback in order to improve our events and services.

I have also created content on multiple social media platforms to better engage with our students. I strive to provide value and utilize our influence to connect with the students.
