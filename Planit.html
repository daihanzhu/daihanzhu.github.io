User Experience Design


Planit is a conceptual mobile app that makes event planning fun and inviting. 

One of the hardest things when it comes to event planning is finding a time that works for everyone. There are countless scheduling apps out there, but they all have one thing in common, either you are free or you are not. 

With Planit, the most unique feature is that you can select different shade of a colour, which indicates your level of availability. Because let’s face it, just because you are free on a Wednesday morning, doesn’t mean it’s a good time for a pool party. 

Beautifully Visualized 


I designed this app with the focus of functionality, and an organized visual experience. 

The idea popped into my head when I was having trouble finding a time to meet for a group project. Once everyone cluttered the group chat with their availability, we decided to use a scheduling app. However, it took a long time to input each date and hour option. Thus creating a frustrating experience for the user. 

By designing a timeline that can be scrolled left or right, users can easily input their availability in a fun and simple way.  

Process


I start my design process by sketching and making prototypes to be user tested. I also tested an array of events to maximize the app’s functionality potential. It was a challenge in determining what features should be included over the course of planning and event.
